infection 25% recovery 25%
infection 25% recovery 50%
infection 25% recovery 75%
infection 50% recovery 25%
infection 50% recovery 50%
infection 50% recovery 75%
infection 75% recovery 25%
infection 75% recovery 50%
infection 75% recovery 75%

the output per iteration is the number of newly infected and newly recovered
